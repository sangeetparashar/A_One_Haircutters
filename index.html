About Us

Welcome to A-One Hairstyling and Aesthetics School, a modern up-to-date facility located in the Brampton, Ontario at the major intersection of Chinguacousy & Bovaird .

We are registered with the ministry of Training, Colleges and Universities Ontario as a private career college under the private colleges’ act.

As a professional school, we strive to provide professional level programs to help our students on the path to a rewarding career.

Cosmetology schools give you an advantage when pursuing a career in the hair-dressing and beauty industry. In a top school, you gain crucial talents and experience that enlarge your resume and launch you into a career.

At A-One, we ensure that each student is taught techniques needed to quickly advance in their chosen field. Our highly trained and respected educators are constantly upgrading their skills to ensure training remains ahead.

Students learn by demonstration, visual aids and hands-on practice. As growth in this industry reaches new heights and new spas and salons open, our students are sought after to fill these positions. The beauty career is challenging and rewarding and personal growth has no boundaries.

Graduates will have an abundance of employment opportunities – Spas, Salons, and Sales and Marketing, Cosmetic firms, Management, Ownership – to name a few. The high quality of standards, the comprehensive courses and clinical practice ensures that graduates are well trained and highly qualified to commence their chosen career.
